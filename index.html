Why are you using this device?

Remember to use your device with intention. Avoid distractions and make sure each use has a purpose, whether it's for work, learning, or staying connected. It's easy to get lost in endless scrolling or mindless browsing, but your time is valuable. Make every interaction with your device meaningful and productive.
